About AF Labs

We Build Tools that Matter

Grads encounter problems everyday. Whether its not knowing which career to pursue or what steps to take, for a long time we've had to figure it out. But there is a better way. By working together we can aggregate data, analyze opportunities, and make any career transition easy.

Founding

A Seven-Question Survey

In 2023, we surveyed Air Force Grads to learn about their career paths and earnings. We shared the results on USAFA's Facebook group and the responses we got were incredible. Demand for more surveys poured in and we launched a Kickstarter to create this website. But we do more than ask questions.

We tasked ourselves to enhance the lives of USAFA grads with software.

Built for Grads, by Grads. ✈️
